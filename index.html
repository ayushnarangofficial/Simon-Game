How to play this Game-

The computer will blink one color at every level, to complete the levels you have to memorize all the colors the computer has blinked in sequence. Eg- If computer blinks 'Red' color at level 1 you have to click on 'Red' color box to pass that level 1, Then if computer blinks 'Yellow' color at level 2, you have to click on 'Red' color from level 1 first then yellow color to finish Level 2, like this the computer will continue to blink one color at every level you have to memorize the sequence and click all the correct colors in sequence.
Can you reach level 10? Give that a Go.
